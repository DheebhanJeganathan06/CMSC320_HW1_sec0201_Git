
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: We might consider using Git to keep track of code changes and collaborate effectively with partners and coworkers.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: One field where data science could be applied to is healthcare. One example of such an application is by predicting patient side effects and readmissions by using medical records.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines branches and maintains the full history, whereas rebasing moves commits from one branch to the top of another branch
